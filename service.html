Frequently Asked Questions

Fix problems sending AOL Mail

If you're unable to send emails through AOL, try the following steps:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Disable any browser extensions or pop-up blockers
  • Check if your internet connection is stable
  • Ensure you're not exceeding AOL's daily sending limit
  • Temporarily disable antivirus/firewall and test again
  • Use a different browser or device to send the mail
If the issue persists, call us at 800-828-3670 for instant assistance.

Minimum system requirements for AOL Mail

To use AOL Mail smoothly, make sure your system meets the following requirements:

  • Operating System: Windows 10/11, macOS 10.14 or later
  • Browsers: Latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
  • Internet: High-speed broadband connection recommended
  • JavaScript & Cookies: Must be enabled in your browser
  • RAM: Minimum 4 GB
